# Understanding 'Vampire' Loads

'Vampire' loads refer to the energy consumed by appliances and electronics when they are turned off but still plugged in. Common examples include TVs, coffee makers, and chargers. These devices can draw power continuously, leading to unnecessary energy waste and increased electricity bills.
