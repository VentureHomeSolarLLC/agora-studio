# Optimizing Solar Panel Usage During Power Outages

During a power outage, maximizing the direct use of solar power can significantly extend battery life. This involves using high-energy appliances like laundry machines and dishwashers during peak sunlight hours, typically between 10 AM and 2 PM, to avoid depleting the battery's stored energy for nighttime use.
