# Using Solar Power During Peak Sunlight Hours

Peak sunlight hours, typically between 10 AM and 2 PM, are when solar panels generate the most electricity. Using high-energy appliances during these hours can maximize the use of solar power directly, saving stored battery energy for when solar generation is not possible.
