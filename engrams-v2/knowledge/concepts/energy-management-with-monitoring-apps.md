# Energy Management with Monitoring Apps

Monitoring apps provided by battery and solar panel manufacturers, such as Tesla and Enphase, offer real-time insights into battery status and energy consumption. These tools are crucial for effective energy management during power outages, allowing homeowners to adjust their usage based on current battery levels and solar production.
