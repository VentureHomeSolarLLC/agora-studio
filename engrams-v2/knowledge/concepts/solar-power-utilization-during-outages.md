# Solar Power Utilization During Outages

During a power outage, homes with solar panels can continue to generate electricity. However, to maximize efficiency and extend battery life, it's crucial to use high-energy appliances during peak sunlight hours (typically 10 AM to 2 PM) and conserve stored battery power for nighttime use.
