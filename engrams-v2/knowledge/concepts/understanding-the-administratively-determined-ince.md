# Understanding the Administratively Determined Incentive (ADI) Program

The ADI program in New Jersey is designed to support residential solar energy production by offering a stable, 15-year cash payment for every megawatt-hour (MWh) of solar energy produced by home solar systems. This initiative aims to make solar energy more financially attractive and predictable for homeowners.
