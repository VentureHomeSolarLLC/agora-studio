# Eligibility Criteria for the ADI Program

To qualify for the ADI program, a residential solar system must be connected to one of New Jersey's four major utilities under a 1-to-1 net metering agreement. The system must primarily serve to offset the home's energy use, and registration through the SuSI portal by a licensed contractor is required.
