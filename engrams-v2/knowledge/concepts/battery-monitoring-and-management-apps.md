# Battery Monitoring and Management Apps

Battery monitoring apps, such as those offered by Tesla and Enphase, provide real-time data on battery status and energy usage. These tools are essential for effectively managing energy consumption during power outages, allowing homeowners to make informed decisions about when and how to use their stored power.
