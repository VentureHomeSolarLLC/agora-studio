# Eligibility Criteria for ADI Payments

To be eligible for ADI payments, a residential solar system must be connected to one of the four major utilities under a 1-to-1 net metering agreement, primarily designed to offset the home's energy use, and registered through the Successor Solar Incentive (SuSI) portal by a licensed contractor.
