# Understanding the Administratively Determined Incentive (ADI) Program

The Administratively Determined Incentive (ADI) program is a financial mechanism designed to support residential solar installations in New Jersey. It provides homeowners with a stable, 15-year cash payment for every megawatt-hour (MWh) of solar energy produced by their home solar systems. This initiative aims to make solar energy more accessible and financially viable for homeowners, encouraging the adoption of clean energy.
