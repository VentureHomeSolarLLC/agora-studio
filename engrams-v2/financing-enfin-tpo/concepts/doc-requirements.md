# Documentation Requirements

## FICO 700+ — No Docs Required

Auto-approval means no additional documentation needed.

## FICO 620-699 — Stated Income Only

Customer provides:
- Estimated annual income (typed into portal)
- Electronic signature confirming accuracy

**No W2, pay stubs, or bank statements required.**

## FICO < 620 — Declined

No documentation path. Customer must:
- Choose different financing (Mosaic loan)
- Pay cash
- Reapply after credit improvement

## Exception Cases

Enfin may request additional docs if:
- Income inconsistent with credit profile
- Manual review triggered by other factors
- System flags potential fraud indicators

In these cases, Enfin will specify exactly what's needed via their portal.
