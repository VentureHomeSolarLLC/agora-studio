# Credit Requirements for Enfin TPO

## FICO Score Thresholds

**TransUnion FICO 9** is used (no waterfall to other bureaus).

| Score | Outcome |
|-------|---------|
| 700+ | Auto-approval, no additional docs |
| 620-699 | Stated income required |
| < 620 | Decline |

## Stated Income Process

When FICO is below 700:

1. Customer enters estimated annual income
2. Checkbox: "I confirm this income is true and accurate"
3. No W2 or pay stubs required
4. System performs soft verification against credit profile

## Why No W2 Required?

Enfin uses alternative credit data. The stated income + credit profile verification is sufficient for their underwriting model.

## Documenting in CRM

Always record:
- FICO score
- Whether stated income was required
- Any manual review flags
