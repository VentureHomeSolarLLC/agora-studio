# Utility Delinquency Check

Enfin uses **Experian** to check for utility payment delinquencies separately from the FICO score.

## What Gets Checked

- Utility payment history (electric, gas, water)
- Past due status
- Missed payment patterns

## Impact Guidelines

| Scenario | Impact |
|----------|--------|
| Past due notices | **OK** — rarely affects approval |
| 1-2 missed payments | Usually OK |
| 10+ missed payments in 12 months | **Potential issue** — may require manual review |

## Key Point

Most utility delinquencies **do not** affect approval. Only significant patterns of non-payment trigger additional scrutiny.

## If Flagged

1. Document the specific issue in CRM
2. Submit application anyway
3. Enfin may request explanation from customer
4. Typically resolved within 24-48 hours
