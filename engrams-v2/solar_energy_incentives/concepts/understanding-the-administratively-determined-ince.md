# Understanding the Administratively Determined Incentive (ADI)

The Administratively Determined Incentive (ADI) is a program designed to support residential solar energy production in New Jersey. It offers homeowners a fixed cash payment for every megawatt-hour of solar energy produced over a 15-year period. This initiative aims to provide financial stability and encourage the adoption of solar energy by guaranteeing earnings from solar production.
