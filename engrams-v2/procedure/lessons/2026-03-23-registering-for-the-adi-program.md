# Registering for the ADI Program

## Scenario
A homeowner is interested in installing a solar system and wants to take advantage of the ADI program's benefits.

## Solution
The homeowner should ensure their system meets the eligibility criteria, then work with a licensed contractor to register their system through the Successor Solar Incentive (SuSI) portal before construction begins. This secures their rate and ensures they're eligible for the 15-year payment plan.
