# Maximizing Solar Panel Efficiency During Outages

## Scenario
During a power outage, a homeowner with solar panels wants to make the most of their solar energy to minimize battery usage.

## Solution
The homeowner should schedule the use of high-energy appliances like laundry machines and dishwashers during peak sunlight hours, typically between 10 AM and 2 PM, to directly use solar power instead of drawing from the battery. This strategy conserves battery power for nighttime use when solar generation is not possible. Additionally, monitoring solar production and battery status through available apps can help in making real-time adjustments to energy usage.
