# How to Prepare for a Complete Battery Drain

## Scenario
During a prolonged power outage, a home's battery storage may deplete entirely. Preparing for this scenario ensures a smoother transition when power is restored, especially with solar energy.

## Solution
When anticipating a complete battery drain, homeowners should turn off all circuit breakers except those dedicated to solar and battery circuits. This minimizes the load on the battery upon sunrise, allowing for a more efficient recharge and reducing the risk of damage to the system.
