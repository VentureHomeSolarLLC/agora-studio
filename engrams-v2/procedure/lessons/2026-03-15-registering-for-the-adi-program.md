# Registering for the ADI Program

## Scenario
A homeowner is interested in installing a solar system and wants to take advantage of the ADI program. They need to know the steps required to ensure their system is eligible and properly registered to receive ADI payments.

## Solution
To register for the ADI program, the homeowner must first ensure their solar system meets the eligibility criteria. Then, they or their licensed contractor must register the system through the Successor Solar Incentive (SuSI) portal before construction begins. This registration secures the ADI rate for 15 years and makes the system eligible for quarterly cash payments based on energy production.
