# How to Prepare for a Power Outage with a Home Battery System

## Scenario
A homeowner with a solar-plus-battery system faces a forecasted severe storm that may cause power outages. They want to ensure their battery lasts as long as possible to keep essential appliances running.

## Solution
The homeowner should start by fully charging their battery before the storm hits. During the outage, they should use energy-intensive appliances only during peak sunlight hours, unplug 'vampire' loads, adjust their thermostat to save energy, and use monitoring apps to keep an eye on their energy usage. Prioritizing essential appliances and preparing for a complete drain by turning off non-essential breakers will also help extend the battery's life.
