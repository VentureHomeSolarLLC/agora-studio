# How to Prepare Your Home Battery for a Power Outage

## Scenario
A homeowner anticipates a power outage due to an incoming storm and wants to ensure their home battery system (e.g., Enphase or Tesla) is optimized to last through the outage.

## Solution
The homeowner should first ensure all 'vampire' loads are unplugged to reduce unnecessary power drain. Next, they should plan to use high-energy appliances during peak sunlight hours if they have solar panels. Adjusting the thermostat to reduce heating or cooling demand and prioritizing essential appliances can also help conserve battery power. Finally, using monitoring apps to keep an eye on energy consumption and battery status will enable more efficient energy management during the outage.
