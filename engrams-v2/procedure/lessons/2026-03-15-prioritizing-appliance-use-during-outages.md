# Prioritizing Appliance Use During Outages

## Scenario
In a power outage, conserving battery life becomes crucial. Homeowners must decide which appliances are essential and which can be temporarily disconnected to save energy.

## Solution
Essential appliances, such as the main refrigerator and internet router, should remain connected but used sparingly. Non-essential devices should be unplugged. This strategy conserves battery power, ensuring that critical needs are met throughout the outage.
