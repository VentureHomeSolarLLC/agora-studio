# Understanding 'Vampire' Loads

'Vampire' loads refer to the energy consumed by electronic devices even when they are turned off or in standby mode. Common examples include chargers, televisions, and kitchen appliances with digital displays. These loads can add up, contributing to unnecessary energy consumption and costs.
