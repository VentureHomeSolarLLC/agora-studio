# Eligibility Criteria for the ADI Program

To qualify for the ADI program, a residential solar system must be connected to one of New Jersey's four major utilities under a 1-to-1 net metering agreement. The system should primarily serve to offset the home's energy consumption, rather than for commercial profit. Registration through the Successor Solar Incentive (SuSI) portal by a licensed contractor is also required.
