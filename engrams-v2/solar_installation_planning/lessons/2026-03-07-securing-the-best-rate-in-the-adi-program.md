# Securing the Best Rate in the ADI Program

## Scenario
A homeowner is considering installing a solar system and wants to ensure they secure the highest possible rate under the ADI program before any potential decreases.

## Solution
To secure the highest rate, the homeowner should engage a licensed contractor to register their system through the Successor Solar Incentive (SuSI) portal before construction begins. This proactive approach will lock in the current rate for 15 years, safeguarding against any future rate decreases due to the program's auto-degression mechanism.
