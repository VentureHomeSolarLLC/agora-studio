# How ADI Payments Work

ADI payments are calculated based on the amount of solar energy produced by a residential system. Homeowners earn one SREC-II certificate for every 1,000 kWh generated, which is then automatically sold at a fixed rate, providing them with quarterly cash payments. This system ensures a predictable income from solar energy production, making it easier for homeowners to plan their finances and investments in solar technology.
