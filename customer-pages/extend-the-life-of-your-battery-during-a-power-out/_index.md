# Extend the life of your battery during a power outage

Tips & tricks to make your battery last as long as possible when the power goes out!

Riding out the storm with an Enphase or Tesla battery during a power outage means being strategic about how you use your power. Here are ways to make your battery last longer, ensuring you stay powered through the outage.

Make the most of sunlight: If you have solar panels, use high-energy appliances like laundry machines and dishwashers during peak sunlight hours, typically between 10 AM and 2 PM. This direct use of solar power saves your battery's stored energy for nighttime.

Turn off 'vampire' loads: Small appliances that draw power even when off, like TVs and coffee makers, should be unplugged. If you have more than one refrigerator, consider using just one to save energy.

Adjust your thermostat wisely: In summer, set your air conditioning a few degrees higher, and in winter, lower your heating a few degrees. Use fans in the summer and extra layers of clothing in the winter to stay comfortable without draining your battery.

Use monitoring apps: Both Tesla and Enphase offer apps that allow you to monitor your battery's status in real-time. These tools can help you manage your energy use more effectively during an outage.

Prepare for a complete drain: If your battery runs out, turn off all breakers except those for solar and battery circuits. This minimizes the load on the battery when it starts recharging at sunrise, ensuring a smoother restart.

Prioritize essential appliances: Keep necessary appliances like your main refrigerator and internet router on, but use them sparingly. Unplug non-essential devices to conserve energy.

By following these tips, you can extend the life of your battery during a power outage, keeping your home powered for longer.
