# What Happens Between Signing and Installation?

After you sign your agreement with Venture Home, there are several steps that need to happen before your installation day. Most customers find this period to be the longest part of the process — **typically around 90 days from signing to installation**, though it can vary depending on your town and utility company.

Here's what's happening behind the scenes and what you can expect.

---

## Your Project Team

Once your agreement is in place, you'll be assigned:

- **Regional Operations Manager**
- **Senior Project Manager**

These team members will oversee your project from start to finish. Our **Customer Success team** is also available throughout this period to answer questions and provide status updates.

> 💡 **You don't need to navigate this process alone** — your team is actively working on your behalf at every step.

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### 1. Custom System Design

Our engineering team will finalize the custom design for your solar system based on:
- Your roof specifications
- Your energy usage patterns
- Your goals and preferences

This design forms the basis for your permits and utility applications, so it needs to be complete and accurate before anything else can move forward.

---

### 2. Permitting and Utility Approvals

This is typically the **longest step** in the pre-installation process and the one most subject to variability.

**What we do:**
- Handle all permitting with your town
- Submit interconnection application with your utility company
- Manage paperwork, submissions, and follow-up

**What you do:**
- **Nothing** — we manage this entirely on your behalf

**Timeline note:** Timelines are set by your municipality and utility. Some towns and utilities move faster than others, which is why the overall timeline can vary.

---

### 3. HOA Approval (If Applicable)

If your neighborhood has a **homeowners association**, Venture Home will work directly with your HOA to obtain any required approvals.

We're familiar with this process and will handle:
- Necessary documentation
- Communication with the HOA
- Follow-up on approval status

---

### 4. Site Survey (If Needed)

In most cases, our team will have everything we need from your initial consultation.

**Occasionally**, a more detailed site visit is required to confirm specific measurements or conditions before finalizing the design.

If this applies to your project, your project coordinator will reach out to schedule a convenient time.

---

- ✅ Permits submitted

- ✅ Installation date confirmed

## What Should I Do During This Period?

**The best thing you can do is simply wait for your approvals to come through.**

Our team is actively managing everything on your behalf. While waiting can feel slow, rest assured that:
- Your project is moving forward
- We're handling all the complex paperwork
- We deal with towns and utilities daily
- We'll reach out if we need anything from you

---

## Timeline at a Glance

| Step | Typical Duration | Your Action |
|------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Custom Design** | 1-2 weeks | None |
| **Permitting** | 4-8 weeks | None |
| **Utility Approval** | 2-6 weeks | None |
| **HOA Approval** | 1-4 weeks | None (if applicable) |
| **Site Survey** | 1 day | Schedule if needed |
| **Total** | **~90 days** | Wait and stay informed |

---

## What Happens Next?

Once all approvals are in place:
1. You'll receive your **installation date**
2. Our crew will arrive and complete the installation (typically 1 day)
3. Final inspection and utility permission to operate
4. Your system goes live!
