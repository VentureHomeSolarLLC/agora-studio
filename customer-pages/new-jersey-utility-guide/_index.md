# New Jersey Utility Guide — JCP&L, PSE&G, Atlantic City Electric

## 1-to-1 Net Metering with Annual True-Up and TREC Incentive

### Net Metering Policy

New Jersey requires all major utilities to provide **1-to-1 net metering** regardless of whether you use the utility's supply service or a third-party supplier.

Each kWh your solar system produces first powers your home; any excess exported to the grid earns a kWh credit that can be used in future months.

---

## Anniversary Month and Annual True-Up

Net metering credits roll forward in a **"NEM bank"** indefinitely throughout the year — but once a year on your **Anniversary Month**, the bank is reset.

### What Happens at True-Up

Any credits remaining at that point are **paid out at a low wholesale rate** (typically 3–5 cents per kWh), **not the full retail rate**.

### Setting Your Anniversary Month

- Defaults to approximately **1–2 months after your system's Permission to Operate date**
- You may change it **one time** by contacting your utility
- **Recommendation:** Watch your bills for at least one full 12-month cycle before requesting a change
- The utility only allows **one change** — make sure you've seen how your credits actually accumulate before deciding

---

## Time of Use Rates — Not Recommended

New Jersey's net metering program is **not well-suited to Time of Use (TOU) rate plans**.

Under TOU:
- Each time period (on-peak, off-peak) has a **separate NEM bank**
- Credits earned in one period **cannot offset charges in another**

This typically results in large on-peak credit balances while paying full price for off-peak consumption.

> 💡 **Recommendation:** Stay on a standard rate plan.

---

## TREC Program

New Jersey's **Transition Renewable Energy Certificate (TREC)** program pays customer-owned solar systems a **fixed per-kWh incentive** for electricity produced.

- TRECs are registered through the **NJ Clean Energy Program**
- Venture Home handles TREC enrollment for all eligible customer-owned systems
- If you haven't received enrollment information, **contact our team**
