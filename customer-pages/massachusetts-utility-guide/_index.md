# Massachusetts Utility Guide — Eversource, National Grid & Unitil

## 1-to-1 Net Metering + Class 1 RECs

### Net Metering Policy

All three major Massachusetts investor-owned utilities offer **full 1-to-1 net metering** for residential solar systems up to **25 kW AC** (limit raised from 10 kW in February 2025).

Each kWh your system exports to the grid is credited against future consumption at the **full retail rate**.

- Credits roll forward **indefinitely** — there is **no expiration** and no annual true-up cash-out
- You simply draw them down when consumption exceeds production (evenings, winter months)

### Fixed Charges

You will still receive a monthly utility bill to cover **fixed customer charges ($10–$15/month)** that cannot be offset by solar. These are **normal and expected**.

## What Venture Home Enrolls You In

Venture Home enrolls Massachusetts customers in the programs appropriate for their system type:

### Customer-Owned Systems (Cash, Loan, Participate Energy)

**Net Metering + Class 1 NEPOOL-GIS RECs**

- You receive retail-rate bill credits for your solar production
- Earn Class 1 RECs for every 1,000 kWh produced, which are sold on your behalf

### TPO Customers (Lease or PPA — SunStrong, Palmetto, etc.)

**Net Metering only**

- Your finance provider retains the REC rights as the system owner

**No action is required from you for either enrollment** — Venture Home handles registration.

> ⚠️ **If you receive an email from NEPOOL-GIS or the program registry asking you to verify your identity, complete it promptly.** RECs accrue from your registration date, so delays mean lost credits.

## ConnectedSolutions — Battery Incentive

Massachusetts battery customers served by Eversource, National Grid, or Unitil are eligible for **ConnectedSolutions**, a demand response program that pays you to reduce grid draw during peak summer periods.

- **Enrollment is voluntary**
- Does not meaningfully reduce your backup capability

**Ask our team about current incentive rates and enrollment when you order a battery.**
