# What Happens If a Panel, Inverter, or Battery Stops Working?

Equipment issues are rare, but they do happen. Here's what to do and what to expect.

---

## Start with Venture Home

**No matter what the issue is:**
- Panel not reporting
- Inverter offline
- Battery not charging
- Error codes in your monitoring app

**Contact Venture Home first.**

You don't need to figure out whether it's a product defect or an installation issue, or call the manufacturer directly.

**We'll:**
- ✓ Diagnose the problem
- ✓ Determine what's covered and by whom
- ✓ Coordinate the repair

**This is true whether your system is brand new or years old.**

**Contact:**
- **Phone:** 800-203-4158
- **App or webchat:** Through the Venture Home app

---

## What's Covered and What Does It Cost?

This depends on what caused the issue and whose warranty applies.

### Venture Home Workmanship Warranty

If the repair falls under our **10-year workmanship warranty:**
- ✅ **No cost to you**
- ✅ We stand behind our installations

See [[warranty-coverage|What's Covered Under Warranty]]

### Manufacturer Warranty

If the issue is an **equipment defect** covered by the manufacturer's warranty (Enphase, Tesla, SolarEdge, etc.):
- ✅ **Equipment replaced at no cost**
- ⚠️ Labor coverage depends on manufacturer's warranty terms
- Some include labor, some cover parts only

> 💡 **We'll be transparent** with you about what applies in your situation before any work is scheduled.

### Out of Warranty

If your system is **outside all applicable warranty periods:**
- We'll give you a **clear estimate** before proceeding
- You decide whether to proceed

---

## TPO Customers (Lease/PPA)

If your system is owned by SunStrong, Palmetto, or another TPO provider:
- ✅ Monitoring, maintenance, and equipment repairs **are included** in your agreement
- ✅ **Still start by contacting Venture Home**
- ✅ We can help triage and connect you with your provider's service team

See [[finance-providers|Finance Provider Information]] for contact details

## What to Expect

**Timeline depends on:**
- Nature of the repair
- Parts availability

**Many inverter and microinverter issues** can be diagnosed remotely.

**We'll:**
- Keep you updated on status
- Give you a realistic timeline once we've assessed the situation
