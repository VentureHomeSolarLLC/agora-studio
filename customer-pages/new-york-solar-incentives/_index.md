# New York Solar Incentives — Complete Guide

## New York State Income Tax Credit — Form IT-255

New York offers a **state income tax credit worth 25% of the cost of your solar energy system**, up to a maximum of **$5,000**.

### How It Works

- Credit is **non-refundable** — can only offset existing New York State income tax liability
- If full credit cannot be used in year of installation, **unused amounts can be carried forward for up to five tax years**

### How to Claim

Complete **Form IT-255** and attach it to your New York State income tax return for the year your system is placed in service.

**Form:** [tax.ny.gov](https://tax.ny.gov)

---

## NYSERDA Residential Energy Storage Incentive

New York State, through NYSERDA, offers **upfront cash incentives** for homeowners who install battery storage systems.

### Incentive Levels

Incentive levels are structured as **$/kWh of installed capacity** and vary by utility territory:

| Territory | Incentive (2025) |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Rest of State** (most Venture Home customers) | ~$200/kWh |
| **Con Edison territory** (NYC and Westchester) | ~$250/kWh |

### How It Works

- Incentives are **paid directly to your installing contractor (Venture Home)**
- Reflected in your **project pricing**
- Available capacity funded in megawatt-hour blocks on **first-come, first-served basis**

**Learn more:** [nyserda.ny.gov/energystorage](https://nyserda.ny.gov/energystorage)

## NYC Solar Property Tax Abatement (Form PTA-4)

**New York City only** — one of the most generous local solar incentives in the country.

### Key Details

- **Value:** 30% of total eligible solar and battery installation costs
- **Applied over 4 consecutive years** at 7.5% per year
- **Annual cap:** $62,500 per year (or your actual property tax liability, whichever is lower)
- **Eligible properties:** Tax Class 1, 2, and 4 NYC properties through December 31, 2034
- **Applies to:** Both solar panels and battery storage installed simultaneously

**Learn more:** [nyc.gov/site/buildings/codes/solar-faq.page](https://nyc.gov/site/buildings/codes/solar-faq.page)

## Statewide Property Tax Exemption

New York State law (Real Property Tax Law § 487) **exempts the added value of solar, wind, and other renewable energy systems from property tax assessments statewide**.

Your solar installation may increase your home's market value — that increase **will not raise your property taxes**.

**This exemption is automatic statewide and does not require a separate application.**

> ⚠️ **Note:** In NYC, the property tax abatement (above) is the applicable benefit and **cannot be combined** with the statewide exemption.

---

## Sales Tax Exemption

Solar energy equipment and installation services are **exempt from New York State and local sales and use tax**.

**No action required** — the exemption is applied automatically at the point of purchase.
