# How Do I Complete My REC Registration? I Got an Email — What Do I Do?

If you've received an email about registering your solar system for Renewable Energy Credits — or if you're wondering why you haven't received one yet — this article explains what's happening and what you need to do.

---

## What the Email Is About

As part of your solar ownership benefits, your system is **eligible to generate Renewable Energy Credits (RECs)** for the clean energy it produces.

**Venture Home handles the registration process on your behalf** — you don't need to initiate anything or find the right registry yourself.

However, at a certain point in the registration process, we need a few things from you directly:
- Identity verification
- Account setup
- Signature confirming your participation

**That's what the email is asking you to do.**

### Who the Email Comes From

The email will come from the **registry or program administrator for your state** — not always from a Venture Home email address directly. **This is normal.**

**The registries we work with:**

| Registry | States Covered |
|----------|----------------|
| **NEPOOL-GIS** | Maine, New Hampshire, Massachusetts, Rhode Island |
| **PJM-GATS** | Maryland, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Delaware |

**Massachusetts and Rhode Island customers** may also receive a separate enrollment email related to the SMART or REG program through their utility.

> ✅ **If you received an email from one of these programs and weren't sure whether it was legitimate — it is.** Complete your portion as soon as you can.

---

## What You Need to Do

The specific steps vary slightly by state and program, but in general you'll be asked to:

1. **Create an account** with the registry using your email address and the information provided
2. **Verify your identity** and confirm your address and system details
3. **Sign or acknowledge** the enrollment agreement

**Time required:** Typically **less than 15 minutes**

Once your portion is complete, Venture Home finalizes the registration on the back end and your system begins accruing credits.

---

## Complete This Promptly

**⚠️ Important:** RECs accrue from the date your system is **registered**, not from the date your system was installed.

**Delays in completing your registration mean:**
- Credits that could have been yours are not being earned
- Lost revenue (in some cases)

**In some programs, registration can be backdated** — but not always, and not indefinitely.

> 💡 **The sooner you complete it, the better.**

## I Haven't Received an Email Yet — What's the Status?

If your system has been live for a while and you haven't received a registration email, a few things may be happening:

### Possible Reasons

- **Your registration is still being processed** on our end and the email hasn't been triggered yet
- **The email went to your spam or junk folder** — check there first
- **We may have an outdated email address** on file for you

### What to Do

**Contact our Customer Success team:** 800-203-4158

**We'll:**
- Look into the status of your registration
- Resend the enrollment email if needed
- Update your email address if it's incorrect

## What Happens After I'm Registered?

Once your system is fully registered, it **begins generating credits automatically** based on your production data.

### By State/Program

#### NEPOOL-GIS (ME, NH, MA, RI)
- Credits accumulate in your registry account
- Can be sold through an aggregator
- **Venture Home can help connect you** with resources for selling your credits

#### PJM-GATS (MD, NJ, PA, DE)
- Credits accumulate similarly
- Can be sold through a broker or aggregator
- **SREC market values vary by state** — New Jersey and Maryland tend to have the most active markets

#### Massachusetts SMART / Rhode Island REG
- Payments flow **directly through your utility bill** as a per-kWh credit
- Once enrolled, this happens **automatically** with no further action required
