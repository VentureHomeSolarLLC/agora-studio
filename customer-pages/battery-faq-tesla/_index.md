# Battery FAQ — Tesla Powerwall

## How long will my Powerwall last during an outage?

This depends on three factors:
1. **Your Powerwall size** (kWh capacity)
2. **Your backup reserve setting**
3. **What you're running** in your home

**The Tesla App shows your estimated backup duration** under **Powerwall > Backup**.

**Power conservation tips:**
- Running lights, refrigerator, router, and phone chargers = **significantly longer backup**
- Running central air conditioning or large appliances = **shorter backup**
- For extended outages: **the more you conserve, the longer your backup lasts**

---

## Can my Powerwall recharge during an outage?

**Yes!**

As long as your solar panels are producing, your Powerwall will **continue charging during a grid outage**.

**On a sunny day this can meaningfully extend** how long you can stay off the grid.

**Where to check:** The Tesla App will show you real-time solar charging even during an outage.

---

## My Powerwall isn't charging — what should I do?

### Step 1: Check for Alerts
Open the Tesla App and check for any **alerts or error messages**.

### Step 2: Confirm Solar Production
- Check the **energy flow screen** to see if solar is producing
- If solar is producing but Powerwall isn't charging → possible issue

### Step 3: Contact Support
If you see an error alert or solar is producing but battery isn't charging:

**Contact Customer Success:** 800-203-4158

> 💡 **Good news:** Tesla systems can often be diagnosed and restarted remotely without needing a technician visit.

---

## Will my Powerwall switch on automatically during an outage?

**Yes!**

The Powerwall **detects grid outages automatically** and switches your backed-up circuits to battery power.

**How fast?**
- Switchover is **nearly instantaneous**
- Most customers describe it as **seamless**
- **No noticeable interruption** to lights or devices

---

## What does "Gateway" mean in the Tesla App?

The **Tesla Gateway** is the **control unit** that manages communication between:
- Your solar system
- Your Powerwall
- The grid

**What it enables:**
- Automatic switching during outages
- Communication with Tesla App
- System monitoring and data

**If you see "Gateway offline" alert:**
→ **Contact Customer Success:** 800-203-4158

---

## Can I charge my Powerwall from the grid?

**Yes!**

**In Self-Powered Mode:**
- Powerwall will draw from grid to maintain backup reserve if solar isn't sufficient

**In Time-Based Control mode:**
- Powerwall will draw from grid specifically during off-peak hours

**Manual charging:**
- Temporarily raise backup reserve to 100%
- Triggers full grid charge
- Useful before anticipated outage
