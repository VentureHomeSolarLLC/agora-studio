# How to Check Your System's Performance

The best way to confirm your system is working is to **log into your monitoring app**. Depending on your system, this will be:

- **Enphase** → Enphase App
- **Tesla** → Tesla App
- **SolarEdge** → mySolarEdge App
- **SunPower** → mySunPower App or SunStrong App

[[monitoring-by-manufacturer|Monitoring Your Solar System by Manufacturer]]

---

## What to Look For

### Inside the App

You can see your:
- **Daily production** — today's generation
- **Monthly production** — this month's total
- **Lifetime production** — total since installation

### Signs Your System Is Working Normally ✅

- ✅ **Consistent daily production** — numbers every day (weather varies)
- ✅ **No gaps in data** — continuous monitoring
- ✅ **No error alerts** — app shows "all clear"
- ✅ **Smooth production curves** — bell curve shape on sunny days

---

## Red Flags to Watch For 🚩

Contact support if you see:

| Red Flag | What It Means | Action |
|----------|--------------|--------|
| **Days with zero production** | System offline or major fault | [[report-issue-service-visit\|Contact support]] |
| **Sudden drop vs. last year** | Possible equipment issue | [[production-low\|Check production guide]] |
| **Error notifications** | Inverter or communication problem | [[system-troubleshooting\|Check troubleshooting]] |
| **Visible panel damage** | Physical damage from storm/debris | [[system-damage\|Report damage immediately]] |
| **Gaps in monitoring data** | Connectivity or hardware issue | [[report-issue-service-visit\|Contact support]] |

---

## When to Contact Support

**Reach out to Venture Home if you see any of the following:**

### Immediate Contact
- 🔴 Multiple consecutive days of **zero production**
- 🔴 **Visible damage** to panels or inverter
- 🔴 **Error alerts** in your monitoring app

### Contact Within a Few Days
- 🟡 **Significant unexplained drop** vs. same month last year
- 🟡 **Gaps in production data** lasting more than a week
- 🟡 **One panel/string** significantly lower than others

### Educational Call (Usually Normal)
- 🟢 Higher utility bill but production looks normal → likely consumption increase
- 🟢 Lower winter production → normal seasonal variation
- 🟢 Day-to-day variation → normal weather effects

## Quick Self-Check

**Ask yourself:**

1. **Is my app showing production every day?**
   - Yes → System is likely working
   - No → Check for errors or gaps

2. **Does sunny day production look like a bell curve?**
   - Yes → Normal operation
   - No → Possible issue

3. **Any red error messages?**
   - No → Good
   - Yes → Note the error code

4. **How does this month compare to last year same month?**
   - Similar → Normal
   - Much lower → Worth investigating
