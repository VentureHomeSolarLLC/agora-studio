# Battery FAQ — Enphase

## How long will my battery last during an outage?

This depends on three factors:
1. **Your battery size** (kWh capacity)
2. **Your backup reserve setting**
3. **What loads are running** in your home

**The Enphase App gives you an estimated backup duration** based on your actual usage history.

**General rule:** Running essential circuits (lights, refrigerator, phone charging, router) lasts significantly longer than running central air conditioning or an electric dryer.

**Where to check:** Battery > Backup Duration in the Enphase App

---

## Can my battery recharge during an outage?

**Yes!** If the sun is shining, your solar panels will continue to charge your battery during a grid outage.

**This is one of the key advantages of pairing solar and storage.** On a sunny day, a well-sized system can recharge your battery enough to extend an outage significantly.

**How it works:**
- Solar panels generate DC power
- Battery charges from solar (priority)
- Battery powers your home simultaneously
- Cycle repeats as long as sun is shining

---

## My battery isn't charging — what should I do?

### Step 1: Check for Errors
Open the Enphase App and look for any **error alerts**.

### Step 2: Check Solar Production
- Look at your solar panel production in the app
- **Low/zero solar + depleted battery** = possible system issue

### Step 3: Check Operating Mode
- Confirm your operating mode in the app
- In **Full Backup Mode**, your battery should be charging aggressively

### Step 4: Contact Support
If everything looks normal but the battery still isn't charging:

**Contact Customer Success:** 800-203-4158

## Will my battery work if the power goes out at night?

**Yes!**

The battery discharges **regardless of whether it's day or night**.

**How it works:**
- Grid goes down at night
- Battery supplies power immediately and automatically
- **Switchover is fast** — most customers report lights don't even flicker
- Battery continues powering backed-up loads until depleted or sun rises

---

## Can I charge my battery from the grid without solar production?

**Yes!**

**In most operating modes:** If your solar production is insufficient to meet your home's needs and maintain your reserve level, your system will **draw from the grid** to keep the reserve topped up.

**In TOU (Time of Use) Mode:** You can program it to charge from the grid specifically during **off-peak hours** when electricity is cheaper.

---

## What's the lifespan of my Enphase battery?

Enphase IQ Batteries are designed to retain meaningful capacity over many years of daily cycling.

**Best practices for longevity:**
- Operate within recommended **80–90% depth of discharge** range
- Set **10–20% backup reserve**
- Reduces stress on battery cells
- Extends overall battery life

**Warranty coverage:**
- **25-year product warranty** covers defects
- Performance degradation beyond specified thresholds
- See [[warranty-coverage|Warranty Coverage]] for details
