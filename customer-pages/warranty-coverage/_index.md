# What's Covered Under Warranty?

Your solar system is backed by multiple layers of warranty coverage — from Venture Home's installation warranty to manufacturer warranties on the equipment itself. Here's a breakdown of what's covered and by whom.

---

## Venture Home Workmanship Warranty — 10 Years

Venture Home provides a **10-year workmanship warranty** on every installation we complete.

### What It Covers

**Installation quality**, including:
- All roof penetrations made during installation
- Structural issues attributable to our installation work
- Roof leaks caused by our installation

### How to Make a Claim

If you experience a roof leak or structural issue that is attributable to our installation work, Venture Home will address it **at no cost to you** within this warranty period.

**Contact Customer Success:** 800-203-4158

### Applies To

All **customer-owned systems** (cash purchase, solar loan, prepaid lease).

> For lease and PPA customers, see the **TPO Coverage** section below.

---

## Equipment Warranties — Customer-Owned Systems

### Solar Panels

- **Duration:** 25-year product warranty
- **Coverage:** Manufacturing defects and performance degradation beyond specified thresholds
- **Provider:** Panel manufacturer

### Enphase Microinverters

- **Duration:** 25-year product warranty
- **Note:** The **Enphase IQ Gateway** (communication hub) carries a separate **5-year warranty**
- **Provider:** Enphase

### Tesla Powerwall (Battery)

- **Duration:** 10-year product warranty
- **Labor:** Tesla's warranty also covers associated labor costs for any covered repairs
- **Provider:** Tesla

### Tesla Solar Inverter

- **Duration:** 12.5-year product warranty
- **Labor:** Also covered by Tesla
- **Provider:** Tesla

### Making Equipment Warranty Claims

If you have a question about a specific equipment warranty claim:

1. **Contact our Customer Success team:** 800-203-4158
2. We'll help coordinate with the manufacturer
3. Manufacturer handles repair/replacement under their warranty terms

---

## TPO Coverage — Lease and PPA Customers

If your system is financed through a **lease or Power Purchase Agreement (PPA)**, your finance provider owns the equipment and is responsible for its maintenance and upkeep.

### Coverage Duration

**Full term of your agreement** — typically **25 years**

### What's Included

- ✅ **All required maintenance and repairs**
- ✅ **System monitoring**
- ✅ **Insurance on the equipment**
- ✅ **Production guarantee** with money-back coverage if your system underperforms

### Your Responsibility

**Nothing.** As a lease or PPA customer, you should **never pay out of pocket** for a system repair or equipment failure.

### How to Request Service

If something goes wrong with your system:

1. **Contact Venture Home:** 800-203-4158
2. **Or contact your finance provider directly**
3. They will coordinate the appropriate service

**Find your provider's contact info:** [[finance-providers|Finance Provider Contact Information]]

## What's NOT Covered

### Customer-Owned Systems

- Routine maintenance (cleaning, inspections)
- Damage from homeowner modifications
- Acts of nature (unless covered by homeowner's insurance)
- Consumable parts beyond warranty periods

### All Systems

- Damage from:
  - Homeowner alterations
  - Third-party work on the system
  - Extreme weather events (may be insurance claim)
  - Neglect or misuse

### For Venture Home Workmanship

**Phone:** 800-203-4158

**Phone:** 800-203-4158

**Contact:** Your finance provider directly
**Or:** Venture Home can route the request: 800-203-4158
