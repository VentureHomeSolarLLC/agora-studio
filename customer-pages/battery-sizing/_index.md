# How Is a Battery Sized?

There's no one-size-fits-all answer. The right battery size depends on two things:

1. **What you want to back up**
2. **How large your solar system is**

---

## What You Want to Back Up

**Whole-home backup:**
- Requires more capacity
- Powers everything (AC, dryer, all circuits)
- Higher cost, shorter backup duration

**Critical circuits only:**
- Requires less capacity
- Powers essentials (lights, refrigerator, router)
- Lower cost, longer backup duration

> 💡 **Most Venture Home battery installations back up critical circuits**, not the entire home.

---

## Your Solar System Size

- **Larger solar system** → can recharge battery faster during outages
- **Smaller solar system** → may need larger battery to get through cloudy periods

---

## The Conversation That Drives the Recommendation

When you contact us, it helps to think about:

- What would you want powered during an outage?
- How long do outages typically last in your area?
- Do you have medical equipment or other critical needs?
- What's your budget?

**We'll design the system around your specific goals.**

---

## Common Battery Sizes

| Battery | Typical Backup (Essentials) | Best For |
|---------|---------------------------|----------|
| **10-13 kWh** | 12-24 hours | Most homes, critical circuits |
| **20-27 kWh** | 24-48 hours | Larger homes, longer outages |
| **40+ kWh** | 2+ days | Whole-home backup, medical needs |

**With solar charging:** Duration extends significantly on sunny days
