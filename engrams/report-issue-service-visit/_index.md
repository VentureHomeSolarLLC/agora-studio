# How Do I Report an Issue or Request a Service Visit?

If something doesn't look right with your system — an error in your monitoring app, a panel that appears damaged, a system that has stopped producing, or anything else that concerns you — our Customer Success team is here to help.

---

## How to Reach Us

We offer multiple ways to get in touch:

### Webchat
Through the chat window on our website or in the **Venture Home app**

### Email
Contact us at our customer support email address

### Phone
Call our Customer Success team directly: **800-203-4158**

### Venture Home App
Submit a service request directly through the app

---

## What Information to Provide

When you reach out, it helps to have a few things handy:

**Required:**
- Which system you have (Enphase, Tesla, SolarEdge, or SunPower)
- Description of the issue

**Helpful:**
- Any error message or alert shown in your monitoring app
- When you first noticed the problem
- Any recent changes (storms, electrical work, WiFi changes)
- Photos of any visible damage

> 💡 **The more detail you can share upfront, the faster we can diagnose the problem.**

---

## What Happens Next

### Step 1: Initial Review
Once we receive your request, our team will review the issue.

### Step 2: Remote Diagnosis
In many cases we can **diagnose and resolve problems remotely** by accessing your system's monitoring data.

### Step 3: Service Dispatch (If Needed)
If the issue requires a technician on-site, we will **dispatch one as quickly as possible** based on the nature of the service needed.

### Step 4: Ongoing Updates
We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

## Common Issues and Response Times

| Issue Type | Typical Response | Resolution Method |
|------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Connectivity/Monitoring** | Same day | Usually remote |
| **Error Codes** | 1-2 business days | Remote or technician |
| **Physical Damage** | 1-3 business days | Technician visit |
| **Zero Production** | Same day | Priority technician |
| **Panel Damage** | 2-5 business days | Technician visit |

---

## What to Expect During a Service Visit

If a technician is dispatched:
- You'll receive a scheduling call
- Adult must be present during visit
- Visit typically takes 1-3 hours
- Technician will diagnose and repair if possible
- If parts needed, follow-up visit scheduled

---

## Emergency Issues

**Call immediately if:**
- Burning smell from electrical equipment
- Sparks or visible electrical hazard
- System physically damaged after storm
- Water leaking near electrical components

**Emergency line:** 800-203-4158
