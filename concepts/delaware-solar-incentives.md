# Delaware Solar Incentives — Complete Guide

## Pennsylvania SREC (AEC) Market Access

While Delaware does not operate its own SREC market, **Delaware solar owners are eligible to register their systems in Pennsylvania's Alternative Energy Credit (AEC) market**.

### How It Works

- You can **earn AECs** for every MWh your system produces
- Sell them to **PA utilities and electricity suppliers** meeting the Pennsylvania AEPS
- AEC prices **fluctuate with market conditions**

### How to Participate

Venture Home can assist with **GATS registration** to enable AEC creation for Delaware customers.

---

## No Sales Tax

Delaware has **no general state sales tax on any purchases** — so solar equipment and installation are automatically free from sales tax.

This is not a solar-specific incentive but represents **real upfront savings** compared to neighboring states.

---

## Property Tax

Delaware generally **does not impose property taxes on personal property**, and in practice solar panels installed on residential properties are treated as personal property equipment.

As a result, solar installations in Delaware **typically do not increase property taxes**, though this can vary by county.

> ⚠️ **Consult your local assessor for your specific situation.**
