# Refer a Friend and Get Rewarded

If you love your Venture Home system, one of the best things you can do is share it. Our referral program pays you for every friend, family member, neighbor, or colleague you refer who gets installed — with **increasing rewards the more you refer**.

There's **no cap, no expiration, and no complicated process**.

Here's how it works.

---

## The Rewards

| Referral | Your Reward |
|----------|-------------|
| **1st successful referral** | **$500** |
| **2nd successful referral** | **$750** |
| **3rd and every referral after** | **$1,000 each** |

### How and When You Get Paid

- **Timing:** Approximately 30 days after your referred friend's system is fully installed
- **Payment method:** Venture Home app or gift card (your choice)
- **No annual reset:** Your referral counter builds for life
- **No cap:** Once you hit the third referral, every successful referral pays $1,000

> 💡 **Example:** Refer 5 friends who all install = $500 + $750 + $1,000 + $1,000 + $1,000 = **$4,250 total**

---

## How to Submit a Referral

**For your referral to count, we need to receive it directly from you before or at the time your friend engages with us.**

### Three Ways to Submit

#### 1. Venture Home App ⭐ (Fastest)
- Submit a referral directly through the app
- Ensures it's logged in our system immediately
- **Recommended method**

#### 2. Phone
- Call us at **800-203-4158**
- Let us know you'd like to submit a referral

#### 3. Email
- Send your friend's name and contact information to **referrals@venturehome.com**

### ⚠️ Important

**Your referral must be registered in our system to earn credit.**

If your friend reaches out to us independently without your referral being logged first, **we won't be able to credit you retroactively**.

> 💡 **Submit the referral as soon as you know your friend is interested — don't wait.**

---

## How It Works

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **You submit** your friend's name and contact information
2. **Our team reaches out** to your friend for a consultation
3. **Your friend signs** an agreement and gets their system installed
4. **Approximately 30 days after installation**, your reward is paid

### That's It!

- ✓ No codes to track
- ✓ No fine print about timing windows
- ✓ No expiration on pending referrals

**If your friend takes a year to pull the trigger, your referral still counts when they do.**

---

## A Few Things to Know

### Anyone Can Refer

You **don't need to have a fully installed and live system** to participate.

If you're still in the pre-install process, you can refer right now.

### One Referral Per Address

Each referred property counts as **one referral** regardless of:
- How many people live there
- Who makes the final decision

### Service Area Required

Your friend needs to be in our service area.

**Venture Home serves:** The Northeast and Mid-Atlantic

**If your friend is outside our footprint:**
- We unfortunately won't be able to move forward
- There's no penalty
- The referral doesn't count against you

### No Cap and No Expiration

- **Refer as many people as you want**
- **For as long as you want**
- A referral you submit today is still valid two years from now

## Who's Worth Referring?

### The Best Referrals Are

Homeowners who:
- ✓ **Own their home**
- ✓ Have a **reasonably sized roof** with decent sun exposure
- ✓ Have a **utility bill worth offsetting**

### Great Candidates

- Someone who's been **complaining about their electric bill**
- Someone **thinking about an EV** (electric vehicle)
- Someone who **recently bought a home**
- Someone who's **mentioned going solar**

### Neighbors Are Often the Easiest

**Why neighbors work well:**
- They've seen your system in action
- Similar sun exposure and utility rates
- Can literally see the results on your roof
- Many of our best customer relationships started with a driveway conversation
