# Washington, D.C. Solar Incentives — Complete Guide

## DC Solar Renewable Energy Credits (SRECs)

The District of Columbia has **one of the most valuable SREC markets in the United States**.

### Why So Valuable?

DC's Renewable Portfolio Standard (RPS) has:
- Aggressive solar carve-outs
- Steep Alternative Compliance Payments

This drives **high SREC demand and prices**.

### How It Works

Venture Home registers your system to earn and track **DC SRECs** through PJM-EIS GATS.

- You **retain the RECs** and sell them through a broker or directly to buyers
- **Market prices can change** — ask your project manager about current pricing at the time of your installation

---

## Property Tax Exemption

Washington, D.C. **exempts the added value of solar installations from property tax assessments**.

Installing solar panels **will not increase your DC property tax bill**.

**The exemption is applied automatically.**

---

## Sales Tax

Washington, D.C. **applies the 6% sales tax** to solar equipment and installation.

**No exemption currently available.**
