# Maine Solar Incentives — Complete Guide

## Solar Property Tax Exemption

Maine **exempts renewable energy equipment** — including solar arrays, inverters, and paired battery storage — from property tax assessment.

### Legal Basis

The exemption was established by **LD 1711 (2019)** and applies to all residential solar installations connected to the grid or used for on-site consumption.

### Coverage

The exemption applies to the **full value of the qualifying solar and storage equipment**, and there is **no time limit** on how long it lasts.

**No action required** — the exemption is automatic.

---

## Sales Tax Exemption

Maine **does not charge sales tax on solar energy equipment purchases**.

The **5.5% state sales tax is automatically waived** for:
- Solar panel systems
- Inverters
- Racking
- Related components

**No action is required** — the exemption is automatic.

---

## Class 1 NEPOOL-GIS Renewable Energy Credits (RECs)

Maine systems can earn **Class 1 RECs** through NEPOOL-GIS.

### How It Works

- **Venture Home handles registration** on your behalf
- RECs are generated based on actual electricity production
- **1 REC per 1 MWh produced**
