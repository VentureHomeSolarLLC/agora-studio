## Common Utility Concepts

### Net Metering
Your system produces → Powers your home → Excess exports to grid → Earns credits → Credits offset future grid usage

### Annual True-Up
When remaining credits are either:
- **Paid out** at reduced rate (NJ, PA, MD, DC)
- **Expire** (DE)
- **Carry forward** (CT, MA, NY, RI)

### Fixed Charges
Monthly fees ($10–$30) that **cannot be offset** by solar credits. You always pay these.

### SRECs vs RECs
- **SREC** = Solar Renewable Energy Certificate
- **REC** = Renewable Energy Certificate (broader)
- Earned per 1,000 kWh (1 MWh) produced
- Sold to utilities to meet renewable requirements
