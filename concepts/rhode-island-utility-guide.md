# Rhode Island Utility Guide — Rhode Island Energy

## 1-to-1 Net Metering with ConnectedSolutions Battery Incentive

### Net Metering Program

Rhode Island Energy offers **1-to-1 net metering** for customer-owned solar systems.

Each kWh your system exports earns a credit at **nearly the full retail rate** — you lose only the renewable energy and energy efficiency charges (approximately 2.2–2.5 cents per kWh).

### Dollar Credits (Not kWh)

Excess credits roll forward indefinitely on your account as **dollar credits** (not kWh).

### Optional Cash-Out

Starting February 2024, you can optionally request a **cash-out of accumulated credits** at the lower Last Resort Service supply rate, or transfer credits to another account in your name.

### Fixed Charges

You will still receive a monthly bill for **fixed customer charges** that cannot be offset by solar.

---

## ConnectedSolutions — Battery Incentive

Rhode Island Energy's **ConnectedSolutions** program pays battery customers for reducing grid draw during peak demand periods.

### How It Works

- **Performance-based payment** per average kW curtailed during dispatch events
- Events are **summer-weighted**
- The program **does not call events** during outages or in the two days before predicted severe weather
- **Enrollment is voluntary**

## RECs

Rhode Island solar customers are eligible for **Class 1 NEPOOL-GIS RECs**.

Venture Home handles registration for eligible customer-owned systems.
