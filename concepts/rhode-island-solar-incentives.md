# Rhode Island Solar Incentives — Complete Guide

## Class 1 NEPOOL-GIS Renewable Energy Credits (RECs)

Rhode Island customer-owned systems earn **Class 1 RECs** registered in NEPOOL-GIS.

### How It Works

- **Venture Home handles registration** for eligible systems
- RECs are earned at **1 per MWh of production**
- Can be **sold to utilities** meeting Rhode Island's Renewable Energy Standard

---

## Property Tax Exemption

Rhode Island **exempts the added value of solar installations from property tax assessment**.

### What This Means

Installing solar can increase your home's market value — in Rhode Island, that increase **will not raise your property taxes**.

### How to Apply

**No application is required**; the exemption is applied automatically when your property is assessed.

---

## Sales Tax Exemption

Solar equipment and installation costs in Rhode Island are **exempt from the 7% state sales tax**.

### How It Works

- Exemption is applied **at point of purchase**
- **No action required**
- On a typical $17,000 system, this saves approximately **$1,190 upfront**

---

## Rhode Island Renewable Energy Fund (REF) Grant

You may be eligible for a **one-time upfront grant** from the Rhode Island Renewable Energy Fund, administered by the Rhode Island Office of Energy Resources (OER).

### How It Works

- **Venture Home handles the application process** as part of your installation
- **Confirm with your project manager** whether the program has available funding at the time of your installation
- Funding availability varies

---

## REG Program Note

Rhode Island customers may also be enrolled in the **REG program** (Renewable Energy Growth), which provides a **fixed per-kWh payment** over a set term, coordinated with your utility at the time of interconnection.

Once enrolled, this happens **automatically** with no further action required.
