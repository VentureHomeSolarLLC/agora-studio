# Pennsylvania Solar Incentives — Complete Guide

## Pennsylvania Alternative Energy Credits (AECs)

Pennsylvania participates in a **Solar Renewable Energy Certificate (SREC) market** — called **Alternative Energy Credits (AECs)** in Pennsylvania.

### How It Works

- Your solar system earns **one AEC per MWh of electricity produced**
- AECs can be **sold to utilities and electricity suppliers** who need them to meet Pennsylvania's Alternative Energy Portfolio Standard (AEPS)

### Market-Based Pricing

Unlike New Jersey's fixed SREC-II program, **Pennsylvania AEC prices fluctuate with market supply and demand**.

> 💡 **Delaware homeowners can also access the PA SREC market.**

### How to Participate

Venture Home registers your PA system in the **PJM-EIS GATS tracking system** so your AECs are created and available for sale.

- You can sell through a **broker or aggregator** to utilities
- Ask your project manager about **current market pricing** and your options for monetizing PA AECs

---

## Property Tax

Pennsylvania **does not have a formal statewide property tax exemption** for solar.

However, because PA property tax assessments are based on property values as of January 1 of the prior year, and reassessments are not triggered automatically, **many homeowners find that their solar installation does not immediately or materially affect their property tax bill**.

> ⚠️ **This can vary by county** — consult your local assessor or a tax professional for your specific situation.

---

## Sales Tax

Pennsylvania **applies the 6% state sales tax** to solar equipment and installation.

**No exemption currently available.**
