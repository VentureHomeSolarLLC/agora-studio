# New Jersey Solar Incentives — Complete Guide

## SREC-II — Successor Solar Incentive (SuSI) Program

New Jersey has **one of the most lucrative solar incentive programs in the country**.

### How It Works

Under the SuSI program's **Administratively Determined Incentive (ADI)** track, residential solar owners earn **SREC-II certificates** that are redeemed for a **fixed, guaranteed payment**:

- **SREC-IIs are separate from your net metering credits** — you earn both
- Venture Home registers your system with the New Jersey Clean Energy Program (NJCEP) and the PJM-EIS GATS tracking system on your behalf
- Payments are made through **InClime**, the SREC-II administrator, once your system is certified

### Value

Fixed payment per SREC-II (varies by project size and type — contact your project manager for current rates).

**Learn more:** [njcleanenergy.com/susi](https://njcleanenergy.com/susi)

## Property Tax Exemption

New Jersey law **exempts the added value of solar installations from local property taxes**.

Your solar system increases your home's market value, but that increase is **fully excluded from your property tax assessment**.

### Savings

With New Jersey's average effective property tax rate of approximately **2.3%**, this saves the typical homeowner around **$1,000 per year** on property taxes.

### How to Apply

To ensure the exemption is properly applied, **contact your local property tax assessor after installation** and confirm your solar system certification is on file.

---

## Sales Tax Exemption

New Jersey provides a **complete exemption from state sales tax** for all solar energy equipment, including:
- Panels
- Inverters
- Mounting hardware
- Battery storage

**Applied automatically at the point of sale** — no application needed.
