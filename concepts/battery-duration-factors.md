# How Long Will My Battery Last?

This depends on three main factors:

1. **Your battery size** (kWh capacity)
2. **Your backup reserve setting**
3. **What loads are running** in your home

---

## The Biggest Factor: What You're Running

**The single biggest factor** is whether you're running:
- ❌ **Air conditioning**
- ❌ **Electric heat**

These are **high-draw appliances** that can drain a battery in a matter of hours.

**Much less demanding:**
- ✅ Lights
- ✅ Refrigerator
- ✅ Router
- ✅ Phone chargers

These can stretch a battery through **a full day or more**, especially with solar recharging during daylight.

---

## Check Your Estimated Duration

**Your app shows an estimated backup duration** based on your home's usage history.

### Where to Find It

| System | Location in App |
|--------|----------------|
| **Enphase** | Battery > Backup Duration |
| **Tesla** | Powerwall > Backup |

### Check This in Advance

> 💡 **Check this before an outage occurs** — not during one — so you have realistic expectations.

---

## Quick Reference

| Usage Level | Approximate Duration | Examples |
|-------------|---------------------|----------|
| **Essentials only** | 12-24+ hours | Lights, fridge, router, phones |
| **Moderate use** | 6-12 hours | Add TV, some outlets |
| **Heavy use** | 2-6 hours | AC, electric heat, dryer |

**With solar charging on sunny days:** Duration can extend significantly

---

## Tips to Extend Battery Life During Outage

- **Turn off non-essential lights**
- **Avoid high-draw appliances** (AC, electric heat, dryer)
- **Charge phones during day** when solar is producing
- **Open fridge minimally** to keep food cold longer
- **Use natural light** during daytime
