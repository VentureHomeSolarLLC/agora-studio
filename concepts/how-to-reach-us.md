# How to Reach Us: Make Sure You're Calling the Right Number

If you've been trying to reach Venture Home and haven't been able to get through, there's a good chance you may be calling a personal cell phone number rather than our main office line.

**This is a common issue** — customers sometimes save a number from an early conversation with a sales representative or project manager and call that directly, not realizing it goes to an individual's personal phone rather than our Customer Success team.

---

## Our Main Customer Support Line

### 800-203-4158

**This is the number that reaches our Customer Success team directly** — the people who can actually:
- ✓ Look up your account
- ✓ Answer questions about your system
- ✓ Schedule service visits
- ✓ Help you with anything post-installation

**Staffed during business hours** and is always the right first call for any support need.

---

## Other Ways to Reach Us

If you prefer not to call or can't get through by phone, we have several other channels that connect you to the **same Customer Success team**:

### Venture Home App ⭐ (Fastest)
- Submit a message or service request directly through the app
- Our team will follow up promptly
- **Most reliable for fast response**

### Webchat
- Use the chat window on our website
- Real-time support during business hours

### Email
- Reach our team at the customer support email address on file in your account

---

## A Note on Response Times

Our team works hard to respond to every inquiry quickly.

**If you haven't heard back within one business day:**
- Try a second channel
- Occasionally messages get missed
- **We'd much rather you reach out again than wait and wonder**

**Most reliable ways to get a fast response:**
1. Venture Home app
2. Main phone line (800-203-4158)

---

## Working With a Specific Team Member?

If you've been working closely with a:
- Regional Operations Manager
- Senior Project Manager
- Other install team member

**And want to follow up with them directly** — that's completely fine.

**But for general support:**
- Billing questions
- Service requests
- Ongoing issues

**Our Customer Success line is always the better path.**

**Why:**
- ✓ Your request is logged and tracked
- ✓ Assigned to someone who can help
- ✓ Won't sit in an individual's voicemail
