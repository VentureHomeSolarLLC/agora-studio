# Venture Home State & Local Solar Incentives Overview

A guide to additional state-level tax credits, SREC programs, battery incentives, and property tax exemptions available across your state — on top of your net metering credits and any Class 1 RECs.

> ⚠️ **Eligibility:** Most incentives require **customer-owned systems (cash or loan)**. TPO customers (lease/PPA) should speak with their financing provider about what applies.

## By Incentive Type

### Property Tax Exemptions

All Venture Home states except Pennsylvania offer property tax exemptions for solar:
- **MA:** 20 years
- **RI, CT, ME, MD, DC:** Full exemption
- **NH:** Most municipalities (check locally)
- **NY:** Statewide (NYC has special abatement)
- **PA:** No formal exemption (varies by county)
- **DE:** Generally exempt (personal property)

### Sales Tax Exemptions

All Venture Home states except Pennsylvania and DC exempt solar from sales tax:
- **MA:** 6.25% saved
- **RI:** 7% saved
- **CT:** 6.35% saved
- **NY:** Full exemption
- **NJ:** Full exemption
- **MD:** 6% saved
- **NH, DE:** No sales tax (automatic savings)
- **PA:** 6% applies
- **DC:** 6% applies

### Income Tax Credits

Only three states offer income tax credits:
- **MA:** 15% up to $1,000
- **NY:** 25% up to $5,000
- **Others:** None

### SREC/REC Programs

| State | Program | Structure | Typical Value |
|-------|---------|-----------|---------------|
| **MA** | Class 1 RECs | Market-based | Varies |
| **RI** | Class 1 RECs | Market-based | Varies |
| **NJ** | SREC-II | Fixed payment | Guaranteed |
| **PA** | AECs | Market-based | Fluctuates |
| **MD** | SRECs | Market-based | ~$50-55/SREC |
| **DC** | DC SRECs | Market-based | High value |
| **DE** | PA AEC Access | Via PA market | Fluctuates |

### Battery Incentives

## Important Notes

### Customer-Owned vs. TPO

- **Customer-owned (cash/loan):** Eligible for most incentives
- **Lease/PPA:** Check with your finance provider; incentives typically go to owner

### Timing

- **Income tax credits:** Claimed the year system is placed in service
- **Property tax exemptions:** Applied automatically (confirm with assessor)
- **Sales tax exemptions:** Applied at point of purchase
- **SRECs:** Earned monthly based on production
- **Battery incentives:** Typically applied to project cost

### Registration

**Venture Home handles registration for:**
- NEPOOL-GIS (MA, RI, NH, ME)
- PJM-EIS GATS (NJ, PA, MD, DE)
- SREC-II (NJ)
- State battery programs (CT, NY, MD)
